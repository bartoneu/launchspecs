# Product Hunt — Launch tags (419 enumerated)

The canonical list of Launch tags accepted by the PH submission form. Compiled from the live form's tag selector as of the date noted at the top of this skill. Total: **419 unique tags** (249 top-level + 170 sub-tags under 30 parent tags).

In PH's data model these are called "Topics". The same enum powers `producthunt.com/topics`. **Categories** (`producthunt.com/categories`) is a separate, smaller curated taxonomy used for discovery — not what you select here.

## Selection rules

- You select **1 to 3** tags per launch (form-enforced).
- Both **parent tags** and **sub-topics** are valid selections (e.g., `Audio` and `Music` are both pickable).
- PH guidance: *"Choose up to 3 launch tags that strongly relate to your launch."* Pick **2-3 tightly relevant** tags, not 3 stretches.
- The selector is **fuzzy/semantic** — searching "video" surfaces `Photo & Video`, `Video Streaming`, `Webcam`, etc. Use this to find the closest match for unconventional product types.

## Notes for tag selection

- Many tags double as **brand-name pages** (`Photoshop`, `Sketch`, `reddit`, `Notion`, `LinkedIn`, `Twitter`, `Telegram`, `Snapchat`, `WordPress`, `Drake`, `DJ Khaled`, `Kanye West`, `Pokemon`, `Star Wars`). These are valid selections only when the product is genuinely about/for that brand.
- **Productivity** has 14 sub-tags — by far the richest group. Productivity-adjacent products should consider sub-tags like `Notes`, `Task Management`, `Time Tracking`, `Storage`, `Slack`.
- **Games** taxonomy is heavily fragmented (29+ game-related tags). Pick the most specific genre.
- **Sports** ≠ **Sports Games** — they're separate top-level tags.
- The corpus may include rare additional tags not surfaced by my enumeration. For mainstream products the 419-tag list is comprehensive; for highly niche tags, fall back to using the form's fuzzy search to confirm.
